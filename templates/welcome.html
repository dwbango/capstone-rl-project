Welcome to the Blackjack RL Project

This project evaluates Q-Learning and SARSA Reinforcement Learning aLgorithms ability to solve the Blackjack problem, providing automated simulations, performance comparisons, and in-depth analytics.

Mission Statement:

The goal is to research, compare, and evaluate diverse RL methods for Blackjack, offering an educational and analytic environment to gain insights into optimal strategies, house edge implications, and long-term expected outcomes.

Explore the RL Simulation link to configure and run automated strategies. You can also visit Free Play Blackjack if you wish to test basic gameplay or see strategy outcomes in real time. Make sure to check the Help section or login for advanced features.

Thank you for visiting, and enjoy uncovering the intricacies of Reinforcement Learning in Blackjack!
